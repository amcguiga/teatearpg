TeaTeaRPG

A monthlyish event hosted by Alex McGuigan in his apartment. We drink tea and play indie RPGs.

About TeaTeaRPG

I'm Alex McGuigan, an Astoria NYC based side-hustler. I organize mini-RPG festivals in Queens and help run an arts mutual aid group, Queens Collaborative. I've been playing RPGs for more than 20 years, starting when I was 10 years old.

Before I started TeaTeaRPG, most of my RPG experience had been in traditional adventure systems like D&D, World of Darkness, GURPS, and d20 Modern. However, I'd long been curious about different types of RPGs; from low crunch adventures like FATE, narrativist games like Apocalypse World, or worldbuilding games like Microscope. I'd accumulated quite a library of different indie RPGs, I just needed to find people to play them with. I decided that I'd host tea parties, open to the public, with a new tea and a new RPG in the pot every time and it grew from there!

I primarily focus on self-published RPGs that can be completed in one session. They typically have small rulesets, do not require a traditional Game Master role, and/or are designed to be played fresh with every game. Often they are not adventure games; I look for games that have interpersonal, worldbuilding, romantic, horror, or philosophical game themes.

For every game, I put one of my favorite teas in the pot. Usually these are camellia sinensis teas from China, Vietnam, Korea, or Japan. We'll have a short discussion about the tea and then take it through its whole steep cycle during the game!
